About us

AWARD WINNING ECOMMERCE MARKETING AGENCY

We’re a diverse, close-knit team of Amazon consultants,
SEO experts, web developers, graphic designers
& content writers

When hiring our team members, we look for their ability to get intrinsically involved in a client’s account & enjoy the process of making an Amazon seller profitable.

Aspiring to be a Center of Excellence for Amazon services, we encourage our associates to see themselves as eCommerce consultants. We reward skills acquisition and eCommerce expertise through programs such as Saturday School (peer-to-peer training sessions conducted every other Saturday), Knowledge Base (an intranet portal where experiences, how to & practical tips are logged) and Achievements DB (our staff log client success stories where they played a part in the achievement).

Not surprisingly, Amazon sellers have rated eStore Factory as 5 stars across multiple categories on Amazon SPN. On Upwork, we have a collective revenue of over $250k, where six of our associates have been awarded the coveted “Top-Rated” badge.

While we are fortunate to be rated so highly by these independent agencies, what really ticks us are the milestones that we help our clients achieve every day. We are convinced that being an eCommerce seller is the most profitable business if done correctly & consistently.

Inhouse staff

We do not pass on our client’s work to freelancers. We have a team of 60+ employees working at our 3 offices. For less than an in-house salary, you will have a team of experts at your disposal.

Constantly evolving

The area we are working in is changing and expanding every day. We ensure that each of our team members is constantly working on their core skills and delivering better than yesterday.

Client-focused

We are not a big organization where our clients are simply numbers for us. We opt for a personalized approach to ensure that you get complete attention and full-stack resources from start to end.

Transparent

You will never be locked in a long-term contract or find billing surprises while working with us. Our month-to-month agreements are flexible, safe and transparent.

Partner, not agency

You don't need another agency; we don't want you to be just another client. We prefer you think of us as a growth partner, collaborator, and wise counselor. The person you call for guidance and advice.

Talented team

We’re a distributed global team with offices in India, the USA, Australia and Germany. This means we have access to industry-leading talent and technology.
